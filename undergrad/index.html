About

This is an undergraduate student seminar on geometry and topology. Here is a course description for you to get an idea of how it runs and particularly for those of you who wish to register for credit. If you would like to propose a topic in geometry and topology for now or the future, write to Yifei Zhu. A possible topic for Fall 2025 is geometric models and their additive engineering, such as this and this. We have 3D printers available thanks to support by the Department of Mathematics and the National Center for Applied Mathematics Shenzhen. The actual theme of the seminar will depend on the interests of the participants.
