Applied and Computational Topology Seminar

  • Southern University of Science and Technology

About

This semester, we aim to understand and experiment on two closely related aspects of applying topology to deep learning: (1) topology of neural network architectures and (2) topological inputs for designing neural networks. We meet Thursdays at 2 pm in M4009.
