About

This semester, our discussion takes a concentrated form of Topological Data Analysis and Deep Learning Discussion Group on subsequent work to the paper Topology-enhanced machine learning for consonant recognition and on topological analysis and deep learning of human speech data. We meet Thursdays biweekly at 3 pm in M5024. Meanwhile, we relegate general seminar talks on applied and computational topology to the Graduate Topology Seminar.

Conference

There will be a Great Bay Area Topology Conference on topological data analysis and deep learning from November 22 to 24, 2024 at SUSTech. All are welcome.
